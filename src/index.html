# About

The GoldenHack is a business hackathon held at Wilfrid Laurier University under the Lazaridis School of Business and Economics on October 5-6, 2019. We are accepting more than 250 students come to Laurier to code, design and innovate their way in our multidisciplinary event. They will solve fun and challenging problems over 24 hours, and we’d love you to be a part of it! We are inviting not only student developers, but also design and business students to encourage well-rounded teams.

# More About

We’re calling ourselves a “business hackathon” to help blend the worlds of business and technology. We often find that although many other hackathons aim to have attendees of all skill sets and programs, many business and design students can feel under-appreciated or even unwanted. Since Wilfrid Laurier University prides itself in it’s business program, while also having its own computer science and user experience design programs, we decided that it was time to bring the technology to the business students, rather than dragging the business students to the technology.
